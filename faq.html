Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about MarketReady.

General
What is MarketReady? +
MarketReady is an AI-powered product listing generator. It creates SEO-optimized titles, bullet points, descriptions, and keywords for 8 marketplaces: Amazon, eBay, Walmart, Etsy, Shopify, Ozon, Wildberries, and Avito. What takes hours by hand, MarketReady does in under 30 seconds.
How does it work? +
Simply enter your product URL or description, select the target marketplace, and our AI analyzes top-performing listings in your category. It then generates a complete, publish-ready listing with optimized title, 5 bullet points, product description, and backend keywords — all in about 30 seconds.
What makes MarketReady different from other tools? +
Three things: (1) We support 8 marketplaces including Ozon, Wildberries, and Avito — most competitors only cover Amazon. (2) Our AI analyzes actual top-performing listings in your category, not generic templates. (3) We generate complete listings in seconds, not just keyword suggestions.
What languages are supported? +
MarketReady generates listings in English and Russian. English for Amazon, eBay, Walmart, Etsy, Shopify. Russian for Ozon, Wildberries, Avito. You can also request both languages for the same product.
Marketplaces
Which marketplaces are supported? +
We support 8 marketplaces: Amazon, eBay, Walmart, Etsy, Shopify, Ozon, Wildberries, and Avito. Each listing is optimized specifically for the target platform — different marketplaces have different requirements for titles, descriptions, and keywords.
Can I generate listings for multiple marketplaces at once? +
Yes! You can select multiple marketplaces in the order form and we will generate a separate optimized listing for each one. The same product gets different titles, bullet points, and keywords tailored to each marketplace algorithm.
Do you support Amazon in different countries? +
Yes. We can generate listings for Amazon US, UK, CA, DE, and other Amazon marketplaces. Just specify the target country in the order form notes.

One listing = one product for one marketplace. If you generate listings for the same product on Amazon and Ozon, that counts as 2 listings. Each listing includes the title, bullet points, description, and keywords.
